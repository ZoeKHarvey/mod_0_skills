Class: Gym sport ball

Attributes:
Color (string)
Material (string)
Total_owned (integer)
In_use (integer)
Firmness (integer)

Methods:
Calculate_worth (uses color and material to decide whether it's still useful)
Calculate_missing (negates in_use from total_owned to calculate any missing)
Bounce (uses firmness and material)
Inflate (uses firmness to calculate need for inflation)
