Class: Gym sport ball

Attributes:
Color (string)
Material (string)
Firmness (float))
Date_bought  (datetime)

Methods:
Calculate_worth (uses color, material and date_bought to decide whether it's still useful)
Bounce (uses firmness and material)
Inflate (uses firmness to calculate need for inflation)
Bouyancy (uses material and firmness to calculate bouyancy)
